## What Is This?

Open Arbor is the professional operations layer of the [OpenArbor Alliance](https://arboracle.app) — an open-source, locally sovereign platform for tree care professionals, ecological stewards, and citizen scientists.

This is not a SaaS product. This is an **instrument**.

> *"An instrument you own. An instrument you play. An instrument that works offline, in the field, where the real work happens."*

Open Arbor gives professional arborists and tree care companies a full business operations platform — CRM, estimates, invoicing, scheduling, tree inventory, ecological valuation — built on open standards, running on your own data, governed by the people who use it.

## Core Features

### Professional Operations
- **CRM** — Client management, contact history, property records
- **Estimates & Invoicing** — Digital approval, PDF generation, payment tracking
- **Job Scheduling** — Crew assignment, recurring jobs, route optimization
- **Materials & Equipment** — Inventory management, maintenance logs

### Tree Intelligence
- **Tree Inventory** — DBH, height, canopy, TIZ/CRZ mapping
- **Ecological Valuation** — Carbon sequestration, stormwater, air quality, biodiversity quantification
- **AI Species ID** — iNaturalist-powered identification with confidence scoring
- **Risk Assessment** — Structural defects, pest/disease, priority triage
- **Ordinance Intelligence** — 3,000+ municipal tree codes, permit requirements, protected species

### Citizen Science Bridge
- **Two-sided flywheel** — Citizen observations feed professional workflows. Professional assessments validate citizen data.
- **iNaturalist Integration** — All species identifications linked to the [Arboracle iNaturalist Project](https://www.inaturalist.org/projects/arboracle)
- **The Soil Grower's Path** — Progression from Seedling Steward to Legacy Protector for every user

### Decentralized by Design
- **Your Data, Your Node** — Records live on your instance, not our servers
- **Offline-First** — Full functionality in the field without cell service
- **Zero Lock-in** — Export everything. Fork the code. Run your own node.
- **Federated Intelligence** — Nodes share aggregated ecological metrics without exposing private business data

## The iNaturalist Connection

Every species identification in Open Arbor connects to the broader biodiversity network.

- The [Arboracle iNaturalist Project](https://www.inaturalist.org/projects/arboracle) aggregates all tree observations from the platform
- Professional assessments create verified observations that strengthen the taxonomic backbone
- Citizen Science observations in the field are validated against iNaturalist's global database
- Species range data, phenology, and ecological relationships flow back into the platform

James Green of [Green Tree Co](https://arboracle.app/NurseryCustomerPortal?nurseryId=6926fcb68a52b3a091e9f328) documents every native species he propagates. That data lives here.

## Philosophy

We are moving from **SaaS** (Software as a Service) to **SaaI** (Software as an Instrument).

The arborist who has spent 30 years reading trees doesn't need an AI to replace their judgment. They need an instrument that captures what they already know, connects it to what others know, and makes the invisible visible — the carbon stored, the water held, the species supported, the value created.

> *"AI as the bicycle, not the replacement. You are the Captain."*

This is the STIM Protocol: **Stasis Through Inferred Memory**. The platform learns from every interaction and grows more intelligent over time — not by replacing the steward's knowledge, but by preserving it.
